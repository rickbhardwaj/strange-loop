Building the Automated Firm

Strange Loop partners with a small number of leading financial services firms to build proprietary AI agents.

These agents automate financial workflows involving unstructured documents, labyrinthine spreadsheets, complex calculations, and firm-specific expertise.

Each AI agent is purpose-built for and wholly owned by the firm, accumulating institutional knowledge and becoming a durable competitive advantage.

Careers

As AI becomes increasingly capable of professional work, the highest-leverage opportunity for exceptional engineers is no longer building software tools, but rather to embed within leading firms and rearchitect their operations around AI.

As a result, we’re looking for a rare kind of engineer: someone who combines engineering rigor, product intuition, business judgment, and customer-facing charisma. Someone who can sit alongside executives and domain experts, identify leverage, and ship high-impact systems at extraordinary speed.

In other words: former founders, future founders, and engineers who think like owners. We’ve built an engineering organization around this type of person:

  • Ownership. No salespeople, no product managers, and no layers of management. You own the customer relationship and work directly with executives at leading financial services firms.
  • Autonomy. Remote-first, complete tool freedom, and minimal process. We optimize for speed and judgment, not committees. Our goal is to reach $100M ARR with 20 engineers.
  • Upside. We share profits across the team equally, with no cap. Our goal is for every engineer to earn $1M+ annually by 2027.
