About Us

We're a team of AI engineers from organizations like Amazon, Google, and UC Berkeley. We embed inside firms and work with experts end-to-end: designing the product, integrating with existing systems, and supporting it in production.

Our AI agents are built for financial workflows involving unstructured documents, labyrinthine, spreadsheets, complex calculations, and firm-specific logic.

Each AI agent is owned by the client and runs on infrastructure they own, becoming a durable firm differentiator.

We partner closely with a small number of firms, typically one per practice. Our goal is to help these firms become the leading firm in their market.
